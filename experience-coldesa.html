Production Manager & Mechanical Designer

Industrias Coldesa

2021 - 2022

Overview

Industrias Coldesa is a leading engineering firm that provides custom solutions for the vertical transport of freight and people. The company specializes in the end to end design, fabrication, assembly, and maintenance of a wide range of products, including industrial and residential elevators, hydraulic lifts, and industrial carts.

Key Responsibilities

  • Operations & Supply Chain Management: Directed end to end plant operations, managing all suppliers of raw materials and services, and supervising the complete lifecycle of project setups from fabrication to assembly.
  • Process & Systems Improvement: Championed and implemented SOLIDWORKS PDM and Office 365 to centralize project data, enhancing information flow and ensuring full traceability across 6 core departments while significantly reducing errors.
  • Team Leadership & Development: Led, trained, and managed a team of 15 electromechanical technicians across manufacturing, installation, and maintenance, fostering a culture of continuous improvement.
  • Design & Process Standardization: Took charge of a company wide initiative to standardize engineering designs and production processes, resulting in improved efficiency, consistency, and quality.

Key Achievements

Established a new production line for aluminium components, achieving a 20% cost and lead time reduction by eliminating outsourcing.
