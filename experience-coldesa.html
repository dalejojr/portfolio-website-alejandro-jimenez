Company Overview

Industrias Coldesa is a leading engineering firm that provides custom solutions for the vertical transport of freight and people. The company specializes in the end-to-end design, fabrication, assembly, and maintenance of a wide range of products, including industrial and residential elevators, hydraulic lifts, and industrial carts.

Company Website: https://coldesa.com/

My Contribution

My role evolved from designing custom lift systems to leading the entire production process. As Production Manager, I focused on improving the systems that support our engineering work. I championed the implementation of SOLIDWORKS PDM to centralize all project data and led a company-wide initiative to standardize our engineering designs, resulting in significant gains in efficiency and quality.

Key Responsibilities

  • New Product Prototyping: Designed the complete prototype for a new Stair Lift product, and then led the hands-on fabrication, assembly, and technical testing processes to validate the design.
  • Systems Implementation: Championed and implemented SOLIDWORKS PDM to create a single source of truth for all project data, enhancing information flow and ensuring full traceability across 6 core departments.
  • Design & Process Standardization: Took charge of a company-wide initiative to standardize engineering designs and production processes, resulting in improved efficiency, consistency, and quality.
  • Custom Mechanical Design: Designed custom freight elevators and scissor lift tables to meet specific customer requirements, preparing all drawings and technical documentation for production.
