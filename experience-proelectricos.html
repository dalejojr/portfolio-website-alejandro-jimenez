June 2016 - August 2017

Overview

As a Mechanical Design Engineer at Proeléctricos, I was responsible for the end-to-end design and development of mechanical enclosures for complex electrical systems, from initial concept and prototyping to final manufacturing documentation for both low and medium-voltage systems.

Key Responsibilities

  • Advanced 3D Design & Modeling: Engineered detailed electrical panels, transformer shelters, and switchgear using SolidWorks, leveraging advanced sheet metal, weldments, and parametrisation.
  • Design for Manufacturing (DFMA): Systematically applied DFMA principles to all projects, optimising designs to streamline assembly and reduce production costs.
  • Prototyping & Iterative Design: Led the full development cycle for new prototypes, which involved creating the initial 3D designs, leading the hands-on fabrication, gathering technical feedback, and iteratively refining the design.
  • Product Line Standardization: Led a key project to standardize residential power meter panels using SolidWorks design tables to create a consistent design that simplified manufacturing.
  • Technical Documentation: Produced comprehensive manufacturing packages, including detailed models, bills of materials (BOMs), and exploded assembly views.

Key Projects & Achievements

Medium-Voltage Switchgear (AIS) Prototype

Led the mechanical design for a new product line, solving complex engineering challenges to allow the company to enter a new market.

  • Explosion Mitigation: Designed a pressure relief system to safely manage internal arc faults, complying with IEC 62271-200.
  • Operator Safety: Developed a fail-safe mechanical interlocking system to protect personnel from live components.
  • Material Innovation: Created a new fabrication process for rigid polycarbonate safety screens through systematic testing and data analysis.

Modular Meter Cabinet

Standardized the meter cabinet product line using SolidWorks, leading to the design of a new modular prototype that became a bestseller in the Colombian market.
