El talECO Project

March 2021 - November 2021

Sustainable Delivery Concept

I founded El talECO, a sustainable grocery delivery concept aimed at reducing plastic waste through a closed-loop system using reusable bags. My goal was to create an eco-friendly alternative for consumers and build a brand from the ground up.

My Role & Responsibilities

  • Brand Development: Registered the brand and created the entire brand identity and marketing strategy.
  • Product Design: Designed the reusable bags, focusing on recycled materials, durability, and user-friendly features.
  • Business Planning: Conducted market research, developed financial projections, and created the complete business plan.
  • Operations & Logistics: Managed initial production, inventory, sales channels, and customer distribution.
