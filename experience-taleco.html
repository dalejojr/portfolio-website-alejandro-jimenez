El talECO Project

March 2021 - November 2021

Sustainable Delivery Concept

I founded El talECO, a sustainable grocery delivery concept aimed at reducing plastic waste through a closed-loop system using reusable bags. My goal was to create an eco-friendly alternative for consumers and build a brand from the ground up, managing everything from product design to business strategy.

My Role & Responsibilities

  • Brand Development: Registered the brand and created the entire brand identity and marketing strategy.
  • Product Design: Designed the reusable bags, focusing on recycled materials, durability, and user-friendly features.
  • Business Planning: Conducted market research, developed financial projections, and created the complete business plan.
  • Operations & Logistics: Managed initial production, inventory, sales channels, and customer distribution.

Key Achievements

Winner, Capital Semilla

Secured seed funding and formal entrepreneurship training after winning a competitive startup grant.

Brand Creation

Developed the entire brand from scratch, including identity, marketing strategy, and product design.

Successful Launch

Achieved over 1,000 units sold during the initial launch phase, validating the business concept.
