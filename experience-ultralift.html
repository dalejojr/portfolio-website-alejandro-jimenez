Ultralift (Australia)

Overview

As a Mechanical Designer at Ultralift, my primary responsibility is to engineer custom mechanical systems for high end residential and commercial projects, focusing on creating products that are reliable, well made, and seamlessly integrated into the customer's environment.

My Role & Responsibilities

  • Advanced Mechanical Design: I design and develop complex mechanical systems in SolidWorks, applying advanced techniques in sheet metal, weldments, and parametric modeling.
  • Product Development: My role involves driving continuous product improvement, which includes refining existing designs and conceptualizing new solutions to enhance performance, aesthetics, and ease of installation.
  • Cross Departmental Collaboration: I maintain a close partnership with the sales, production, and assembly teams, ensuring we align on project goals to deliver high quality products.
  • Production Documentation: A key part of my job is creating and managing all production documentation, such as Bills of Materials (BOMs) and assembly guides, to guarantee clarity and precision for the manufacturing team.
