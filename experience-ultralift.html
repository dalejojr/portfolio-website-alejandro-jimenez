Company Overview

Ultralift is a leading engineering firm in Australia, specializing in precision-engineered solutions for TV, projector, and camera handling systems. The company excels in designing and manufacturing custom products for both residential and commercial applications, ensuring high-quality performance and seamless integration.

My Contribution

At Ultralift, I translate custom client requirements into precise, production-ready 3D models that serve as the blueprint for manufacturing. My role is to bridge the gap between concept and production, ensuring every design is innovative, reliable, and efficient to build.

Key Responsibilities

  • Advanced 3D Modeling & DFM: I translate custom client requirements into precise, production-ready 3D models using SOLIDWORKS, applying Design for Manufacturing (DFM) principles to ensure every design is optimized for efficient assembly.
  • Product Development in SOLIDWORKS: I actively contribute to product innovation by refining existing designs and developing new concepts entirely within SOLIDWORKS to improve mechanical functionality, aesthetics, and ease of installation.
  • Collaborative Design: The SOLIDWORKS model serves as the central point for collaboration. I work closely with sales and production teams, using 3D designs to align on client needs and ensure technical feasibility before manufacturing begins.
  • Manufacturing Documentation from 3D Models: I create all essential production documentation directly from the master SOLIDWORKS models, including accurate Bills of Materials (BOMs) and clear, visual assembly guides.
